# Voice Guide — Guardrails

Read `references/writing-samples.md` FIRST for voice. This file is guardrails only — structural rules, anti-slop, quality checks. Not voice creation.

## Writing Modes

Match the mode to the story's emotional weight:
1. **Nghiêm túc, logic** — analytical, evidence-driven. Humor restrained, logic front and center
2. **Nghiêm trọng cợt nhả** (DEFAULT) — serious topic, playful delivery. Escalation then deflation
3. **Tự trào phúng, triết lý** — self-aware humor landing on philosophical insight

## Material Test

Before writing or rewriting, test the material:
- Does it have genuine tension or conflict?
- Is there a reversal or surprise?
- Would a friend care at dinner?
- Two-sentence test: still interesting?

Fails the test? Stays as dev note. "Nói nhiều nói dai là nói dại."

## Anti-Slop Rules

### Banned Phrases
- Throat-clearing: "Here's the thing:", "It turns out", "In today's [X]", "At its core"
- Emphasis crutches: "Full stop.", "Let that sink in.", "Make no mistake"
- Adverbs to kill: really, just, literally, genuinely, honestly, simply, actually, deeply, truly, fundamentally, inherently, inevitably
- Filler: "It's worth noting", "At the end of the day", "When it comes to"
- Meta-commentary: "Plot twist:", "Let me walk you through..."
- Telling not showing: "This is genuinely hard", "actually matters"

### Structural Patterns to Kill
- Binary contrasts ("Not X. But Y.") — just state Y
- Negative listing ("Not a X... Not a Y... A Z.") — just say Z
- Dramatic fragmentation ("[Noun]. That's it. That's the [thing].")
- False agency ("the decision emerges") — name the actor
- Passive voice — find the actor, make them subject

### Rhythm Rules
- Base rhythm is **flowing, not fragmented**: 70% medium sentences, 20% long, 10% short accents
- Data lives **inside** prose, not isolated as fragments
- Paragraphs breathe: 3-5 sentences building one thought
- Start sentences with "And", "But", "So" — humans do this
- Uniform sentence lengths are the biggest AI tell

## Vietnamese Anti-Slop
- Kill connector spam: "Ngoài ra", "Hơn nữa", "Bên cạnh đó"
- Vietnamese connective tissue is NOT filler: mà, thì, rồi, cơ mà, cho nên — keep them
- Use discourse particles: thôi, đấy, nhỉ, nha, vậy, hem
- Vietnamese modifiers are natural: hơi, khá, cũng, vẫn — keep them
- No heavy nominalization: "làm" not "việc thực hiện"
- No "Tóm lại" closing — end forward
- Don't default to formal register
- Rhetorical questions are valid Vietnamese transitions

## What to Avoid
- Corporate voice, passive voice, vague declaratives
- Over-explaining — trust the reader
- Forced structure, forced humor
- Mixing registers accidentally
- Writing that sounds pleased with itself — flatten it
- Contractions required in casual: "it's", "can't", "I've"
- Specificity: no number/name/date = rewrite it

## Pre-Delivery Checklist
1. Any adverbs? Kill them
2. Any passive voice? Find the actor
3. Throat-clearing opener? Cut to the point
4. Three consecutive sentences at similar length? Break one
5. Any "Not X. But Y."? State Y directly
6. Vague declarative without specifics? Make it concrete
7. Connector spam? Cut
8. Sounds pleased with itself? Flatten it
9. Score: Directness / Rhythm / Trust / Authenticity / Density (1-10 each, revise below 35/50)
